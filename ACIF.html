System Dynamics

Mental Gravity: How fast your focus drains when task input stops.
Distractions: The volume of random internal/external jitter.

Layer Coupling strengths (\( \beta \))

Goal Power: How much task clarity fuels your flow.
Confidence Power: How much inner certainty fuels your flow.

Inferential Components

Choice Sharpness: How 100% sure you are of your next move.
Thinking Distance: How many steps ahead you are planning.

Neural & Context Layers

Task Clarity: How well your actions actually match your goals.
Brain Flexibility: How fluidly your brain switches modes.
Cognitive Regimes
Flow: Confident action with optimal strategic foresight (\( \tau \approx \tau^* \)). Strategic benefit outweighs mental cost.
Anxiety: "Brain Freeze" via excessive depth (\( \tau > \tau^* \)). Over-thinking the future crashes your inner precision.
Hyperfocus: High confidence but "Myopic" (\( \tau \ll \tau^* \)). Absorbed in the now, but losing the big picture.
Boredom: Shallow planning depth and low task engagement. Insufficient strategic depth to maintain focus.
